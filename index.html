This page is dedicated to Michael Vick

Michael Vick was the Heisman quarterback for Virginia Tech in 1999 who went on to be drafted by the Atlanta Falcons with the 1st overall pick in the 2001 NFL draft.

He went on to play 6 season with Falcons.
